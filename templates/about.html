About Vortex

What is Vortex?

Vortex is an interactive learning platform designed to transform traditional study materials into engaging, intelligent, and interactive formats. Using smart AI tools, Vortex converts PDFs, lecture slides, and notes into summaries, quizzes, flashcards, and mind maps.

Features

  • Smart Summarization: Automatically generates concise summaries from lengthy documents
  • Interactive Quizzes: Creates questions to test your understanding
  • Flashcards: Generates interactive flashcards for quick review
  • Mind Maps: Visualizes key concepts and relationships
  • Gamification: Earn points, unlock achievements, and track progress
  • Dark/Light Mode: Choose your preferred viewing experience

Development Team

Vortex was developed by the Vortex Development Team, Computer Science students at Level 10, University College of Ranyah.
