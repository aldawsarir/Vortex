Help and Instructions

How to Use Vortex
1Upload or Paste Content

Go to the home page and either paste your study material in the text area or upload a file (PDF, DOCX, or PPTX).

2Generate Summary

Click the "Generate Summary and Quiz" button. Vortex will process your content and create a concise summary.

3Review Results

View your summary, interactive flashcards, and mind map on the results page.

4Take the Quiz

Click "Go to Quiz" to test your understanding with automatically generated questions.

5Check Your Score

After submitting the quiz, view your score and achievements on the gamification page. Earn points and unlock new levels.

Tips
  • Use the Dark or Light Mode toggle for comfortable reading
  • Click on flashcards to flip and reveal answers
  • Complete quizzes to earn points and track your progress
  • Use the mind map to visualize key concepts
  • Upload clear, well-formatted documents for best results
