Help & Instructions

How to Use Vortex
1 Upload or Paste Content

Go to the home page and either paste your study material in the text area or upload a file (PDF, DOCX, or PPTX). Make sure your content is clear and well-formatted for best results.

2 Generate Summary

Click the "Generate Summary and Quiz" button. Vortex will process your content using AI and create a concise, easy-to-understand summary in seconds.

3 Review Results

View your summary, interactive flashcards, and mind map on the results page. Explore different ways to learn and retain information.

4 Take the Quiz

Click "Start Quiz" to test your understanding with automatically generated questions. Multiple choice, true/false, and fill-in-the-blank questions are included.

5 Check Your Score

After submitting the quiz, view your score and achievements on the gamification page. Earn points, unlock new levels, and track your progress!

Pro Tips for Better Learning
  • Use the Dark or Light Mode toggle for comfortable reading in any environment
  • Click on flashcards to flip and reveal answers - great for quick review sessions
  • Complete quizzes regularly to earn points and track your learning progress
  • Use the mind map to visualize key concepts and understand relationships
  • Upload clear, well-formatted documents for the best AI processing results
  • Join quiz battles to compete with friends and make learning more fun
  • Check the leaderboard to see how you rank among other learners
